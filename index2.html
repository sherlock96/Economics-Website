Panel Discussions

To capture and assess the economic or financial impact of recent policies and development, the department regularly organises panel discussion with eminent panellists from industry as well as academia. Some of the panellists for intellectual discussions includes Prof. Krishnamurthy Subramanian (ISB, Hyderabad), Prof. Thirmulai Ramabhadran (ISB, Hyderabad), Prof. K Nageshwar, (Osmania University), Prof. Indrakanth (RBI Chair Professor, CSD, Hyderabad). Various issues like “Impact of Union Budget”, “The Great Indian Development - Growth vs. Development”, “Britain Exit – BREXIT” have been discussed.
